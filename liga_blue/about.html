How to get LIGA tokens

Want to participate in the LIGA token airdrop?
Anyone have a chance to receive LIGA token. In order to participate, you will need to:
  • 1
    Follow us on twitter or join our telegram group
  • 2
    Leave your waves wallet address and twitter or telegram account by submitting this form

If you don’t have Waves wallet you can create one here: https://client.wavesplatform.com

Only 200 addresses will get the drop. We will randomly choose addresses and drop 50 LIGA tokens at each address on July 29th

All the players participated in World Cup 2018 and Champions league 2018/19 Tokenization will receive the drop guaranteed with other randomly chosen players. They need to have any team token from the past events on their balance and submit this form.

After the drop the players could trade Liverpool or Chelsea tokens on DEX. Sell orders will be placed on Waves DEX in LIGA tokens.
