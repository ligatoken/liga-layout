What is LIGA

LIGA is a platform with tokenized sport events, enabling you to deal with real-time rates and to trade your personal predictions with others. Based on Waves blockchain.

The idea is to combine suspense we get while watching a big sport event with a trading logic.

LIGA tokenizes big sport events by creating unique token for every team that takes part in a competition. Each team has its own unique ID token and fixed emission. Players can buy this tokens and trade them during the championship.

We don’t control token’s price. Players do.

By selling these tokens LIGA accumulates prize pool. At the end of the championship the prize pool will be distributed between the winning team token holders.
